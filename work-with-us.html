Work With Us

Join a team that values your contribution, supports your growth, and is committed to making a real difference in healthcare.

Join Our Team

Quality care starts with supported and valued people

At Vanguard Health Group, we believe quality care starts with supported and valued people. We are always looking for passionate, skilled, and reliable professionals who share our commitment to integrity, respect, and high standards of care.

We offer flexible opportunities across hospital settings, residential facilities, and community services. Our team is supported through clear communication, strong leadership, and a focus on safety, professionalism, and compliance.

Whether you are a nurse, allied health professional, or a support worker, you'll be part of an organisation that values your contribution and supports your ongoing development.
