About Us

Who We Are

Delivering workforce solutions across Australian healthcare

Vanguard Health Group delivers high-quality, flexible workforce solutions across healthcare and community support. We connect skilled and qualified professionals with organisations and individuals to support the delivery of essential healthcare services across a range of care settings.

Through a strong focus on workforce capability, service continuity, and quality outcomes, we respond to evolving healthcare needs by providing reliable access to nursing, support, allied health, and related services.

Our approach is grounded in high professional standards, compliance with regulatory requirements, and a commitment to safe, effective, and person-focused care. By strengthening healthcare capacity and supporting service providers and consumers, we contribute to improved well-being and the development of healthier, more resilient communities.

Our Story

Why We Founded Vanguard Health Group

Vanguard Health Group was founded by two nurses who have worked extensively across the health, aged care, and community services sectors. Through years of hands-on experience, we consistently witnessed significant gaps in the industry — including unreliable service delivery, poor continuity of care, limited support for carers and nurses, and a lack of accountability for client outcomes.

We saw the impact these gaps had not only on clients and families, but also on frontline workers who were often unsupported, overstretched, and undervalued.

Together, we combine practical, frontline understanding with structured governance and operational excellence — enabling us to deliver services that are reliable, ethical, and truly person-centred.

Mission & Values

Rodolfo Celis Pereira

Co-Founder, Vanguard Health Group

Rodolfo Celis is an experienced healthcare professional with over 15 years of experience across residential aged care, acute care, and community services. He has developed a well-rounded career combining clinical practice, case management, and service coordination, with a strong focus on improving outcomes and quality of life for individuals receiving care.

Rodolfo has extensive experience working in residential aged care settings, where he provided comprehensive nursing care to older adults with complex health conditions. In this environment, he supported residents with clinical care, medication management, and daily living needs, while ensuring dignity, safety, and person-centred care were maintained at all times.

He also contributed to acute healthcare services as part of the nursing bank at Royal Melbourne Hospital, gaining valuable experience in fast-paced clinical environments and strengthening his clinical decision-making and adaptability.

Over the years, Rodolfo transitioned into case management within the aged care sector, supporting clients under the Support at Home and My Aged Care programs. In this role, he has been responsible for coordinating services, developing personalised care plans, managing budgets, and ensuring clients receive appropriate supports to maintain their independence and wellbeing at home. His work involves families, healthcare providers, and allied health professionals, while advocating for client needs and navigating complex care systems. Rodolfo is committed to empowering clients to achieve their goals, promoting dignity, choice, and person-centred care.

In addition to his work in aged care, Rodolfo has also been actively involved in the disability sector, providing support to individuals within the community and facilitating meaningful engagement in daily activities. His approach is centred on inclusion, respect, and enhancing each individual's ability to participate fully in their community.

Passionate about making a positive difference in people's lives, Rodolfo continues to demonstrate a strong commitment to high-quality care, professional integrity, and continuous improvement across both aged care and disability services.

Michele Myla Cruz Bucoy, RN

Clinical Lead, Director & Co-Founder, Vanguard Health Group

Michele is a highly experienced Registered Nurse and Clinical Leader with over 28 years of expertise across Haematology/Oncology, Clinical Research, and community-based services. She brings a strong track record in clinical governance, service development, and leading high-performing care teams across complex healthcare environments.

Her clinical background includes advanced practice and care in Stem Cell Transplantation (BMT), Apheresis in Haematology/Oncology Ward, with previous leadership role as a Haematology Nurse Consultant at Monash Health leading the care of blood cancer patients. Michele has contributed to major Australian healthcare institutions, including The Alfred and Peter MacCallum Cancer Centre, where she played a key role in delivering specialised and evidence-based care.

Since 2021, Michele has been working as a Clinical Lead in the Aged Care and Disability sectors, where she provides strategic oversight, clinical consultancy, and operational leadership to support safe, person-centred care in the community. She works closely with multidisciplinary teams, clients, and families to ensure individuals remain safe, functional, and independent in their homes while navigating complex healthcare and support systems.

Through her leadership in the sector, Michele identified a critical gap in accountability, workforce capability, and consistency in care delivery for vulnerable populations. Michele and her long-time colleague, Rodolfo founded Vanguard Health Group — a service-driven organisation committed to raising the standard of care across aged care, disability, and healthcare settings.

Under Michele's leadership, Vanguard Health Group is focused on delivering high-quality, person-centred care in homes, facilities, and hospitals; addressing workforce shortages by providing skilled, reliable nurses and carers; leading with integrity, accountability, and clinical excellence; supporting clients to access appropriate funding and navigate complex care systems; and collaborating with medical and allied health professionals to optimise outcomes.

Michele's vision is to build a workforce that leads by example, delivering genuine, compassionate, and professional care while improving outcomes for vulnerable individuals and supporting the broader healthcare system.

With a strong focus on advocacy, innovation, and continuous improvement, Michele continues to position Vanguard Health Group as a trusted partner in delivering safe, effective, and high-quality care across the community.
